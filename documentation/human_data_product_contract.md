## Purpose

The Human Data Product Contract explains what this asset is, how it should be interpreted, and what principles govern its use as a professional data product.

This document exists to clarify that the Human Data Product is not intended to function as a conventional resume. It is a structured, queryable, documented representation of professional information.

## Contract summary

The Human Data Product should be interpreted according to the following principles:

### 1. Structured representation
The product organizes professional information as structured entities rather than only narrative text.

### 2. Queryable design
The product is designed to support exploration through read-only SQL, documented APIs, and insight views.

### 3. Data-product framing
The asset is intended to demonstrate how professional identity and experience can be modeled using data-product principles such as:
- normalized data structures
- documented interfaces
- reusable analytical outputs
- traceable relationships across entities

### 4. Evidence-oriented interpretation
Where possible, derived views and capability signals are grounded in structured source records such as:
- projects
- experiences
- skills
- feedback
- role preferences
- normalized capability evidence

### 5. Human interpretation still matters
The Human Data Product is a structured representation of professional experience, not a claim that all professional value can be fully reduced to structured fields or derived analytics alone.
